PRIVACY POLICY — SPARK DECK

Last updated: March 27, 2026

OVERVIEW

Spark Deck ("the App") is a conversation card game designed to help people connect through thoughtful prompts. We believe your conversations are yours — not ours. This privacy policy explains how the App handles your data.

WHAT DATA WE COLLECT

We collect no personal data.

The App does not require an account, login, email address, or any personally identifiable information to function.

WHAT DATA IS STORED ON YOUR DEVICE

The App stores the following data locally on your device using your browser's or device's local storage:

- Game preferences: Your liked, disliked, and skipped cards, so the recommendation engine can personalize your experience.
- Session state: Your current game progress (edition, vibe, energy settings, card position), so you can resume a game.
- Settings: Your last-used configuration per edition (vibe, energy, age range), so setup is faster on return visits.
- Walkthrough status: Whether you've completed the first-time tutorial, so it doesn't repeat.

This data never leaves your device. It is not transmitted to any server, cloud service, or third party.

WHAT DATA WE DO NOT COLLECT

- No personal information (name, email, phone number, address)
- No location data
- No device identifiers
- No usage analytics or tracking
- No crash reports (unless you opt in through your device's OS-level reporting)
- No advertising data
- No data from other apps on your device

THIRD-PARTY SERVICES

The App does not integrate with any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services. There are no ads in the App.

CHILDREN'S PRIVACY

Spark Deck is designed to be used by people of all ages, including children under 13 with their families. Because the App collects no personal data whatsoever, it is compliant with the Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA) and similar regulations.

The App's content is filtered by age range using on-device settings. No age data is transmitted to any server.

DATA DELETION

Because all data is stored locally on your device, you can delete it at any time by:

1. Clearing the App's data in your device settings, or
2. Uninstalling the App

No additional steps are needed because no data exists on our servers.

CHANGES TO THIS POLICY

If we update this privacy policy, we will post the revised version in the App and update the "Last updated" date above. We will not retroactively collect data that was not described at the time of collection.
